About Our Project

Our data provides a visualization of homelessness in the United States over the last thirteen years (2007 – 2019) for each state. Data was extracted from the Housing Urban Development (“HUD”) exchange which shows percentage of homelessness for each state (2007 – 2019), total beds available (Housing Inventory Count) and census population information.

The dashboard shows each state's increases and decreases in homelessness, beds available and percentage of homelessness compared to total state population from the United States Census.

We also analyzed Point-In-Time (PIT) data information available through HUD to determine demographic categories of the homeless population.
